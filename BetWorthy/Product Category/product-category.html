Best Sports Betting Sites in the U.S.

Please keep in mind that although there are a lot of great sports betting sites and apps in the U.S., online sports betting is not legal in every state.

Some states offer more than 20 choices, some may have just one or two options, while others haven’t legalized it yet!

Make sure to check your state laws and regulations before you try to sign up.

That said, here are some of the top options available in multiple states.

BetMGM

BetMGM is a super-organized sportsbook that’s easy to use. Here, live gambling is available for numerous sports, but the main thing that stands out is the props builder engine. Using this feature, you can use stats to customize your bets according to individual player performance.

Bet365

Bet 365 is a great sportsbook that offers more live soccer events than most other sports bookmakers. However, it offers fewer sports betting lines and markets, and it’s not the number one pick if you’re looking to bet on tennis or basketball. Where it excels is with its cash-out feature, which often puts you in profit just a few minutes after you’ve placed your bet. Its user interface, meanwhile, is simple and easy to navigate, and the website streams numerous active games each weekend.

PointsBet

Although the debuted recently, in NJ in 2019, it’s turned out to be one of the most exciting online sports betting sites around. PointsBeet captured a a lot of attention with its recent deal with NBC Sports sportsbook, offers excellent markets and odds, and a straightforward registration process.

Caesars

Caesars is a popular online sports betting website, now available in more than 9 states. After WilliamHill acquisition they are very aggressively growing the market share and becoming one of the dominants online betting operators in the U.S. Its sports coverage is comprehensive, including soccer, basketball, horse racing, and tennis.

BetRivers

Thanks to a retail casino presence in several states, BetRivers have been able to enter to online sports betting market relatively quickly in several states. The company has come to be known for teasing players with solid offering and excellent loyalty program. Plus the whole experience is seamless.

Choosing The Right Online Betting Site

Pick Trusted Sports Betting Sites

The main thing you need to do is pick a sports betting website that’s reputable. In other words, it’s legit, safe and not a scam.  We can’t overestimate this point enough because scam betting sites DO exist.  To find out whether or not a betting site is to be trusted, you can do a few things. First, make sure the site is licensed. For example, all legit U.S. online betting sites are licensed by the local state where they operate. You should be able to find details of a betting site licensing on its homepage.  Second, you can read review sites like ours. We pride ourselves on evaluating sportsbooks and highlighting the best ones that can be trusted, and which are safe to use.

What Makes an Ideal Online Betting Experience?

The thing with the best online betting is that – more than anything else – it should be an enjoyable experience. Whilst winning obviously improves our experience (!), there are other things to bear in mind that will make your experience better, even if your luck is out:

  • A clean website that’s easy to navigate
  • Lots of markets with good odds
  • Live bet opportunities
  • Good cash out options
  • Reliable and speedy withdrawal process
  • Variety of payment options

All of these are essentials that you need to look out for when choosing your next online sportsbook.

Sports You Can Bet Online

Most of the sports betting sites in our guide offer a variety of sports and sports leagues. When choosing your next sportsbook, you must check whether or not it has the rights reserved to offers the sports you want to bet on.  The main sports are, of course, football, soccer, basketball, baseball, tennis, cricket, golf and ice hockey. But if you like to do betting on niche sports, such as curling, skiing, and weightlifting, make sure to check whether or not this sportsbook has them covered carefully. And if they do, are the markets comprehensive? Is in-play betting available? The same goes for sports leagues.

Other Site Features

Many of us sign up for a sport betting site to gamble on sports. But there always comes a time when there aren’t any games worth betting on – yet we still fancy a bet. So what do we do?  It always helps when a sportsbook offers other betting opportunities, such as poker. This way, you can have a play whenever you want without having to wait for the next football game to start.

Creating an Account

Some sites make it super easy to create an account. Others make the whole process horribly lengthy. We’ve verified the sites in our guide, and each one is easy enough to sign up to. Some, such as SBG Global, require you to verify your ID before you can withdraw your money, but all have simplified the sign-up process so that you don’t need to wait too long before your account is up and running.

Deposit Options

More and more sports bettors are depositing funds via Bitcoin, while others use PayPal and others use Neteller. Some still prefer to use credit or debit cards. As such, a sportsbook must have the deposit option you prefer. Otherwise, you’ll be left frustrated when it’s time to make your first deposit. Make sure, then, to check whether or not a sportsbook has your payment method.

Conclusion

Online betting is always better when you pick the right sports betting site. While you’re free to now do your own research before selecting a site (or two) to sign up to, just make sure you check your local laws and regulations first. Whichever site you choose, remember to only gamble for fun. Good luck!
