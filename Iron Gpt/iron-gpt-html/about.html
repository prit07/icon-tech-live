Who We Are

We are Deutsche AI Systems, a division of Deutsche Technologies LLC, a team of experienced engineers and managers with an elite network of technology partners. We are AI enthusiasts and commited to what we do 24/7.

Deutsche Technologies and Deutsche AI Systems are forward-thinking enterprises with German management, dedicated to continuously pushing the boundaries of what's possible in artificial intelligence. We work at the intersection of technology, AI, and human experiences to lay the foundation of a smarter, more connected world.

Assembled under the strategic guidance of Roland Priborsky, the CEO, our team comprises a diverse mix of world-class AI experts, data scientists, engineers, strategists, and creative thinkers. Each member brings to the table a unique perspective and fresh ideas, all united by a common commitment: To develop advanced AI solutions that empower individuals and businesses alike.

Roland Priborsky and his team of specialists have trained and fine-tuned B1, an advanced knowledge bot that is remarkable for its ability to provide educational information and engage users on a wide array of topics. From history, geography, and geopolitics to astronomy, technology, and other sciences, B1 aims to satiate the innate human curiosity and encourage lifelong learning.

We believe that AI is not just about algorithms and code, but about solving real-world challenges and shaping the future. We stay ahead of the curve by fostering a culture of innovation and bold thinking, which ultimately helps us deliver advanced AI solutions that elevate the human experience as a whole.

As we continue to advance in our journey, we remain committed to the pursuit of excellence, ethical AI practices, and innovations that stand the test of time.
