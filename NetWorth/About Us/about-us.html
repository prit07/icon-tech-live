About Fit Small Business

Since founding Fit Small Business in 2013, over 80 Million readers have relied on us to deliver the best answers to their small business questions.

We know that business owners like you wear many hats. This means having to make important decisions in a wide variety of areas where you may have little direct experience. It also means you are time stretched, and need to make those decisions as quickly and efficiently as possible.

That’s why we’re here – to help you make the best decisions for your business, and to do so as quickly and efficiently as possible.

We do this by hiring experts with deep industry experience, and putting our readers at the center of everything we do as a business. That’s what you get here at Fit Small Business. Great advice from true industry experts in each of our topics of focus.

Our Topics of Focus & Industry Experts

Accounting

Timothy Yoder

Tim worked as a tax professional for BKD, LLP before returning to school and receiving his Ph.D. from Penn State. He then taught tax and accounting to undergraduate and graduate students as an assistant professor at both the University of Nebraska-Omaha and Mississippi State University. Tim is a Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor as well as a CPA with 28 years of experience. He spent two years as the accountant at a commercial roofing company utilizing QuickBooks Desktop to compile financials, job cost, and run payroll. Tim has spent the past 4 years writing and reviewing content for Fit Small Business on accounting software, taxation, and bookkeeping.

How We Make Money

We earn money primarily through commissions that are earned when you purchase products and services featured on our website.

We know that earning and keeping your trust is what makes Fit Small Business successful. That’s why 100% of our recommendations are editorially independent. Our industry experts recommend whomever they think is the best option, regardless of any financial relationship. It’s been that way since we founded our site in 2013, and we never plan on changing that.
