What is a VPN, and why do I need one in 2025?

The use of a Virtual Private Network, or VPN, works much like a tunneling procedure to connect your device safely with the internet. In the face of the rising tide of cyber threats-advanced phishing scams, ransomware attacks, and ever-more invasive corporate data scraping-VPNs will be more important in 2025 than ever before. With concerns over personal privacy at an all-time high, a VPN ensures private browsing history, sensitive personal information, and other online activities remain what they should be-private. They are also vital in accessing content blocked by geographical restrictions or bypassing government censorship in some countries.
