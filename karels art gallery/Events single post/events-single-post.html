Haití: Luces y colores del Caribe

Events

Hotel Eurostars Central, Madrid, Spain

Karel’s Gallery, known for its exquisite collection of Haitian Naïve art, is thrilled to collaborate with the Cultural Section of the Embassy of Haiti in Spain and Eurostars Hotels’ Art and Culture Department. Together, we’re delighted to present the exhibition ‘Haiti: Lights and Colors of the Caribbean’ from June 1st to July 17th.

This extraordinary event was inaugurated with the presence of distinguished guests, including His Excellency Mr. Guy Lamothe, the Ambassador of Haiti, His Excellency Mr. Juan Bolivar Diaz, the Ambassador of the Dominican Republic, and His Excellency Mr. Mauricio Carlo Gelli, the Ambassador of Nicaragua. The event also saw the participation of diplomatic representatives from Italy, Honduras, Serbia, and Sudan.

We’re profoundly grateful for the gracious hospitality extended by the Eurostars Hotel management and for the attendance of our cherished art enthusiasts. Thank you for being a part of this memorable occasion.
