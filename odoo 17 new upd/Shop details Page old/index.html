description

The Beluga Belt right-hand chaise longue sofa, designed by Jacobo Ventura, stands out for its contemporary and sophisticated design, inspired by the curved forms of the whale. This modular piece not only brings a touch of elegance to any setting but also offers unique versatility to adapt to various space configurations. Its smooth lines and leather detailing enhance its modern style, making it the ideal addition for those seeking both distinction and comfort in their home. The Beluga Belt right-hand chaise longue is customisable with a wide selection of fabrics, allowing it to suit different tastes and interior styles. With its exclusive design and high-quality materials, this sofa guarantees both functionality and visual appeal. Its versatility and focus on comfort make this piece essential for those looking for the perfect blend of design and comfort in their interiors.
